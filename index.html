Focus on Utility


Legendary Toyota Quality

Offered in two trim levels--SR5 and Limited and with two or four- wheel drive--the 4Runner is powered by a mighty dual-overhead cam 3.4-liter V6 engine paired with an automatic transmission. The engine produces 183 horsepower at 4,800 rpm and 217 foot pounds of torque at 3,600 rpm.

A True Go Anywhere, Do Anything Vehicle

Both the suspension and frame were designed to balance on-road comfort and off-road performance. A coil-spring, double-wishbone front suspension features a closed structure for the lower arm. This design adds strength while minimizing weight. Off-road enthusiasts will also find enhanced low-traction performance available through a locking center differential and a two-speed transfer case. With the center differential lock switch engaged, the front and rear axles are locked together for better grip in low-traction situations.

Most Appealing Compact SUV

A rugged exterior houses a spacious interior with plenty of creature comforts. Full cut-pile carpeting, front four-way adjustable fabric bucket seats and rear 50/50 split folding fabric bench seat successfully blend comfort and utility. An overhead console with a garage door opener/sunglass holder, a center console storage box with dual cupholders and two power outlets in the front and an additional power outlet in the rear cargo area make 4Runner convenient to use as well.
